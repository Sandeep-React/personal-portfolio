(i)Hypertext (ii)Markup Language

Ans. - Hypertext-

Hypertext is a piece of ordinary text that has special feature of linking to other documents or webpages.

Markup language-

Markup language that uses special symbols called Tags to mark up text document that instructs the browser how to dsplay the text.
